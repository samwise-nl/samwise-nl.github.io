Developer Hiring - Resume Do's and Don'ts

February 20, 2020 1-minute read

In my current role as a senior software developer, at a small start-up, I get to look over a lot of resumes - from interns, to junior devs right up to very experienced devs.

Make it a PDF

Not everyone has MS Office. PDF is near-universal and renders in browsers without plugins.

Keep your resume to 2 pages or less

I’m usually going through multiple resumes at a given time and I don’t have time. Tell me what you’ve done succinctly and efficiently.

Use a spell checker

Unless you’re sending your resume in by mail and you’re using a typewriter then you have access to a decent spell checker.
